**Overall insights**:

a). In most maps, players spend the most time on the periphery of the map and there are large areas of the map that remain unexplored. Players also largely extract points in these areas. Two ways to get customers to explore more areas on the map are:

(i). Introduce missions or larger treasure chests in the center of the map to incentivize players to explore these areas

(ii). When entering the map or the game, players can enter the map from different corners to explore more of the map

b). There is very less player-to-player combat so far. Most combat is between bots and players and is restricted to the periphery of the maps. Two ways to introduce more combat into the game are:

(i). Introduce tournaments  for a limited period of time  in the center of the map where players can enter and top leaderboard participants can extract their prize in the form of loot

(ii). Incentivize combat – players who kill other players can collect most of their loot if collected within a specific period of time post kill. Further, reward players who engage in combat regularly through enhanced player appearance in the form of shields/skins/better weapons or a new player metric similar to Health called Courage
